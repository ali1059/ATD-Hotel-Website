About Hotel ATD

In the close proximity from our hotel you can enjoy breakfast, business lunch or romantic dinner. There are many restaurants of European, Japanese, Mediterranean, Caucasus, Russian cuisine, cozy cafes and pastry shops.

The main department stores of the capital for children and adults are situated in close proximity of our hotel. There are the largest bookstore, antique shops, different exhibitions and fairs are held in Gostiny Dvor.
